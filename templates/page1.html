About the Food Data

The data in this website comes from the CORGIS food data set. The website for the data can be found here . The data has information on a variety of different foods and drinks. This information includes the different vitamins and nutrients in each item. The items are also sorted into categories with a description and a nutrient data bank number to set them apart from the other foods and drinks.

The vitamins and nutrients are recorded with different measurements. For example, one of the nutrients is Beta Carotene and this is measured in mcg. Other examples would be protein which is measured in g and calcium which is measured in mg.

Every food item has a unique ID number. This ID number is called the Nutrient Data Bank Number. You can use this website to find different food items by their name or their number.
